DIABETES DIET PLAN

DO

DONT'S
1) STARCH
Eat more wheat bread rotis, brown rice and oats
Whole grain starches give more vitamins and minerals
Avoid white bread, white rice and deep fried foods
Avoid sweets which increase sugar level
2) FRUITS
Eat small servings of apples,bananas and oranges
Avoid processed fruits and fruit products which are
loaded with sugar
3) VEGETABLES
Load up on spinach, tomatoes, green beans,cucumber,
brocoli, cauliflower and sweet potato which are low in starch
Say no to fried vegetables that add to calories and fat in diet
4) PROTEIN
Opt for skinless chicken,fish and lean cut meats instead of red meats
Include some plant based protein like beans and nuts
Avoid eating fatty cuts of meat and processed or frozen meat
5) DAIRY PRODUCTS
Go for unflavoured dahi,milk and paneer rich in calcium
and vitamins
Say no to full fat diary products
6) FATS AND OILS
Opt for vegetable fats and oils such as sesame oil, olive
oil and mustard oil
Stay away from saturated and partially hydrogenated fats
that come from animal
products and plant oils
